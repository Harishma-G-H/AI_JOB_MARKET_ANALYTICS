AI Job Market Analytics


📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes global AI job market trends, salary patterns, skill demand, and hiring insights across industries and countries. The dashboard helps users understand the evolving AI job landscape and identify high-demand skills and career opportunities.

🚀 Features

 - Global AI job opening trends analysis

 - Salary comparison across job categories and experience levels

 - Industry-wise demand for AI professionals

 - Analysis of remote, hybrid, and on-site job opportunities

 - Skill demand analysis for Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing

 - Country-wise AI hiring distribution

📊 Dashboard Insights

 - AI job openings have grown significantly from 2020 to 2026.

 - Senior professionals receive the highest average salaries.

 - Finance, Manufacturing, and Education industries show strong AI hiring demand.

 - Flexible work models (Remote, Hybrid, On-site) are widely adopted.

 - Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing are among the most sought-after skills. 

🛠 Tools & Technologies

  - Power BI Desktop

  - Data Visualization

  - Microsoft Excel

  - DAX

  - Power Query

🎯 Objective
      To provide actionable insights into the global AI job market, helping job seekers, researchers, and organizations make informed decisions regarding AI careers and workforce planning.
